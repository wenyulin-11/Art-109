About

Wenyu Lin is a digital media artist based in the Bay Area, specializing in digital and video art. His work explores the dynamic interplay between abstract forms of expression and digital media, that transforms ideas into visuals. Through his artworks, Wenyu aims to capture and convey emotions, and personal experiences.
